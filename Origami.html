1.Fold the paper in half

2.fold the paper in hlf again

3.Open the top flat of the paper over to the right and squash fold this flap down

4.Turn the paper over and open the top flat to the left and squash fold it down

5.Fold and unfold the sides and the top along the dotted lines.

6.Open up the top layer of paper and fold it up using the creases from the previous step

7.Fold in both sides along the existing creases making a petal fold

8.Turn the paper over and repeat the steps 5-7 on the other side

9.Fold both sides to the center along the lies. then repeat on the other side

10.Inside reverse fold up both sides of the paper just like a paper crane

11.Reverse fold the back part down to make the back legs and fold the top flat of paper down and repeat on the other side to make front legs

12.Reverse Fold the tip down to make the head.

13.Inside Reverse Fold the tip of the head. Fold the back of the leg inside the model along the dotted line. Repeat on the other side.

Our Camel is ready!!!!

4.Open up each corner and Squash Fold them flat. See the next step to see how each corner looks after the Squash Fold.

6.Turn the paper over and fold the 4 flaps of paper out along the dotted lines.

7. Fold the paper in half over to the back.

8.Inside Reverse Fold the paper up along the dotted line.

9.Outside Reverse Fold the other side of the model down along the dotted line to form the tail.

10.Make another Outside Reverse Fold on the end of the tail.
